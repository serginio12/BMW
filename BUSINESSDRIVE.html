The BMW 7 Series surpasses all expectations of luxury driving. Remarkable core features such as the 6-cylinder TwinPower Turbo engine and the fully adaptive and lightweight chassis are elevated by subtle touches of delight; the light carpet and massage seats are perfect examples of this. From every perspective, this is a luxury driving experience of the highest order.

REFINDER FLAIR

Obsessive attention to detail gives The 7 a unique character, from the double crease running down the side to the Hofmeister Kink, which for the first time is incorporated into the window outline. Other refined elements include the chrome-plated trim, integrated exhaust pipes, and the optional rear chrome bar, which accentuates the vehicle’s low centre of gravity.

EXECUTIVE LOUNGE

Step inside The 7 to experience next level luxury. Sensory elements such as ambient lighting and fragrances, as well as hand-crafted materials, are complemented by the latest innovations. These include two 10” TV screens, massage chairs with eight different functions and a bespoke Bowers & Wilkins sound system, made from pure, cultured diamonds.

AFTER MORE?

For added levels of craftmanship, the BMW 7 Series can be upgraded to the Exclusive model, which has the Design Pure Excellence package. Alternatively, it also comes available as a M Sport model, which features a host of BMW M details and 19” light alloy wheels, just to mention a few.
